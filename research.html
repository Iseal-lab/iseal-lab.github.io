Research

Research Areas

Research topics are grouped into separate cards so visitors can understand the laboratory’s scope quickly.

Semiconductor Materials Fabrication, Spectroscopy, and Characterization

Focus on manufacturing techniques, spectroscopy, and characterization of semiconductor materials.

Surface and Interface Engineering

Research on surface/interface passivation and surface coating strategies for improved semiconductor performance and stability.

Bandgap Engineering and Minority Carrier Control

Study of bandgap engineering and minority carrier control for advanced semiconductor and energy-related applications.

Novel Device Prototyping

Prototyping of novel semiconductor devices, including power devices, solar cells, gas sensors, microelectromechanical devices, and photodetectors.
