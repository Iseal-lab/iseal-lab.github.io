Official Laboratory Website

i-SEAL Laboratory

Next-Generation Intelligent Semiconductor Energy Engineering and Analysis Laboratory (i-SEAL) is an international and interdisciplinary laboratory at Kyungpook National University. The laboratory conducts research on semiconductor materials, spectroscopy and characterization, surface and interface engineering, bandgap engineering, minority carrier control, and the prototyping of novel devices.

Team

Open to multiple levels

The laboratory recruits full-time research staff and engineers, postdoctoral researchers, postgraduate students, and undergraduate students.

Vision

Semiconductor energy initiatives
